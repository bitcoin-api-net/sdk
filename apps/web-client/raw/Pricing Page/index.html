Billing & FAQ

Common questions about our pricing model.

Q.

Are public endpoints really free?

Yes. Public endpoints are free to access. However, they are subject to default rate limits to ensure fair usage across all users. Use Rate Boost if you need higher guaranteed throughput for specific endpoints.

Q.

How is Rate Boost billed?

Rate Boosts are billed monthly. The cost is applied per-endpoint, meaning you only pay to increase the capacity of the exact endpoints your application heavily relies on, rather than upgrading to a generic, expensive global tier.

Q.

Can I get invoices for my purchases?

Yes, detailed invoices are available in your account dashboard for all paid plans and individual Rate Boost purchases.
